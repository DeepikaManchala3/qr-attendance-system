QR Based Attendance System

SRKR Engineering College, Bhimavaram

About the College

SRKR Engineering College, established in 1980, is a premier institution located in Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh. It is affiliated with JNTU Kakinada and offers quality education in engineering and technology. The college is well-known for its modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and research-driven approach. Students are encouraged to innovate through labs, projects, and industry collaborations.

Project Description

Our project introduces a QR-based attendance system for students. It records attendance in classrooms, libraries, labs, and hostel entry/exit in an accurate and paperless way. This reduces manual work, saves time, and improves accuracy. Our aim is to build a secure, fast, and user-friendly attendance solution.
